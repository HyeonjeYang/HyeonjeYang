# Present_0206 👋

**Department of Biological Sciences, SNU** — a biologist who keeps reaching for physics.

[![Typing SVG](https://readme-typing-svg.demolab.com?font=Fira+Code&weight=600&size=22&pause=900&color=F7768E&center=false&vCenter=true&width=600&lines=a+genome+is+just+a+very+long+polymer;a+cell+is+just+a+very+small+circuit;currently+modeling+chromosomes)](https://github.com/HyeonjeYang)

### what I'm working on
- **Chromosome biophysics** — I treat the genome as a (very long) polymer and chase how its 3D folding falls out of the physics. Hi-C, P(s) scaling, loop extrusion.
- **Building physical models** of chromatin from the ground up. The one I'm most excited about lives in a private repo for now, so you'll have to take my word for it.
- Stochastic side-quests when I get curious — LEF dynamics, digestion statistics, metapopulation models.

### things I build with my hands
- **Circuits** I design, solder, and code myself (Arduino IR intervalometers and friends)
- **Optics** — homemade refractors and a browser-based ray tracer
- **Astrophotography** — pointing all of the above at nebulae 🔭

### what I reach for
**physics side** &nbsp;`OpenMM` · `polychrom` · `NumPy` / `SciPy`
**biology side** &nbsp;`AlphaFold` · `Foldseek` · `Biopython`
